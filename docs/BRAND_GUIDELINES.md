# Nano Banana Brand Guidelines
> "Reasoning at the Speed of Light"

## 1. Brand Philosophy
**Nano Banana** represents the fusion of playful creativity ("Banana") and precision engineering ("Nano"). Our design language is **Cyber Minimal**: dark, high-contrast, and focused on content, with subtle animated interactions that feel alive.

## 2. Color Palette
We use a restricted palette to maintain a premium feel.

| Role | Color Name | HEX | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Background** | Deep Space | `#09090b` | Main application background |
| **Surface** | Orbital Gray | `#18181b` | Cards, sidebars, headers |
| **Primary** | Violet Nebula | `#8b5cf6` | Accents, buttons, user messages |
| **Success** | Signal Green | `#10b981` | Status indicators, success states |
| **Text** | Starlight | `#fafafa` | Primary headings and body text |
| **Muted** | Moon Dust | `#a1a1aa` | Secondary text, metadata |

## 3. Typography
We use a dual-font stack to separate UI from logical reasoning.

*   **Headings & UI**: `Inter` (Weights: 400, 500, 600)
    *   Clean, legible, modern sans-serif.
*   **Reasoning & Code**: `JetBrains Mono` (Weights: 400, 500)
    *   Technical, monospaced, high readability for complex traces.

## 4. Logo Usage
The Nano Banana logo is a geometric abstraction of a synapse or a node network.
*   **Primary**: Use the Violet Nebula mark on Deep Space background.
*   **Monochrome**: Use pure white on dark backgrounds for high contrast.
*   **Spacing**: Maintain at least 16px clear space around the logo.

## 5. Voice & Tone
*   **Professional, yet accessible.**
*   **Precise, yet not robotic.**
*   **Helpful, transparent, and logical.**
